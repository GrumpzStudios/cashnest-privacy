Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

The short version: Savvable does not collect, store, or share your personal or financial data with anyone. All your data stays on your device and in your private iCloud account. We have no servers and no analytics.

1. Overview

Savvable ("the App") is a personal finance management app developed by Grumpz Labs ("we," "us," or "our"). We built Savvable with a privacy-first architecture. Your financial data never leaves your device except to sync to your own iCloud account.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any data. Savvable does not have servers, databases, or analytics services. We cannot see, access, or retrieve any information you enter into the App.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework:

  • Budget categories and spending limits
  • Expense records (amount, merchant, category, date, notes)
  • Bill information (name, amount, due date, category)
  • Subscription details (name, amount, billing cycle, category)
  • Savings goals and progress
  • Debt balances and payoff plans
  • Income entries
  • Receipt images (stored locally on device only)
  • App preferences (name, notification settings, streak data)

This data is stored exclusively on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. We have no access to your iCloud data.

4. Apple FinanceKit

Savvable uses Apple's FinanceKit framework (iOS 17.4+) to read transaction data from Apple Wallet with your explicit permission. This data is used to:

  • Automatically detect recurring bills and subscriptions
  • Categorize spending for budget tracking
  • Improve cash flow forecasting accuracy

FinanceKit data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service. You can revoke FinanceKit access at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Finance.

5. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, the App uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is protected by your Apple ID credentials. We do not operate any servers involved in this process and cannot access your synced data.

6. Third-Party Services

Savvable does not use any third-party services including:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No financial data aggregators (no Plaid, MX, or Yodlee)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media SDKs

7. In-App Purchases

Savvable offers premium features via Apple's StoreKit. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information. Your subscription status is verified locally through Apple's StoreKit 2 APIs.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose to third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

9. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • On-device encryption: iOS encrypts all app data at rest using hardware-backed encryption
  • iCloud encryption: Data synced via CloudKit is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • No network exposure: The App does not make any network requests to third-party servers
  • Device authentication: Your data is protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID

10. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the App's Settings > Reset All Data option. You can also delete the App, which removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud data, disable iCloud sync for Savvable in your device settings or delete the data from iCloud.com.

11. Children's Privacy

Savvable does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect information from any user.

12. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the effective date. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
